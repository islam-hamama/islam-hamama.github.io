**Period:** April 2022 – December 2022

Weekly English-language seminar covering Python programming with a focus on the **PyGMT** module for scientific mapping and visualization. Topics included:

- Python fundamentals and scientific computing (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib)
- GMT (Generic Mapping Tools) and PyGMT for geophysical mapping
- Practical data visualization for seismological and infrasound datasets
- Workflow automation with Python scripts

Delivered to graduate students at the School of Systems Engineering, Kochi University of Technology, Japan.
