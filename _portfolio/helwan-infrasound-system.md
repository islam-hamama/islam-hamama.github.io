## Overview

Established a **4G full remote-monitoring infrasound station** at Helwan, Egypt, as part of the Egyptian National Data Center's monitoring infrastructure at the National Research Institute of Astronomy and Geophysics (NRIAG).

## Key Features

- 4G cellular network connectivity for real-time remote monitoring
- Continuous infrasound data acquisition and transmission
- Integration with NRIAG's national seismic monitoring network
- Used for monitoring regional and local infrasound sources including quarry blasts, earthquakes, and atmospheric events

## Research Applications

The station has been used in several research studies, including the investigation of mysterious seismoacoustic signals from eastern Helwan quarry blasts (published in *Scientific Reports*, 2025).
